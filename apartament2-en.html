Frequently asked questions

Apartment Bukowska 11a/5

How do I get access to the apartment?

After booking, you will receive an email with the access code to the building and apartment.

Is there an elevator in the building?

There is no elevator in the building. Apartment No. 5 is located on the second floor.

Is parking available?

Public paid parking is available on site, where it is not possible to reserve a parking space. 350 m from the apartment - under the Kaponiera Roundabout - there is a 24-hour, underground paid parking.

Is breakfast included?

The price of accommodation in the apartment does not include any meals.

Can I rent a bicycle?

For a small fee, you can rent a bike as part of the Poznań City Bike service. The rental point is located at Kaponiera Roundabout, 350 m from the apartment.

Is there internet access in the apartment?

In all apartments, guests have free access to wireless internet (WiFi).

What types of payment methods do you offer?

Payment in cash or by card is possible at the apartment reception (Hotel Zielony, 24/7).

Will I receive a VAT invoice?

On request, we issue a VAT invoice and send it by email.

Do you accept animals?

Pets are not allowed.

Is it possible to add extra beds?

There is no capacity for extra beds in the apartments.
